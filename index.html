Terms of Service

Please read this before placing your order.

PAYMENT

  • Full payment IN ADVANCE before work begins.
  • Methods: PAYPAL PAYMENT / Via Vgen (contact me for details).
  • Prices are subject to change depending on the complexity of the work.
  • Additional elements (background, second character, etc.) will incur an extra charge.

REVISSION

  • The number of revisions depends on the selected tier.
  • Major revisions after the sketch is approved will incur an additional fee (to be discussed with the client).
  • Significant design changes are considered an additional fee (to be discussed with the client).
  • Please provide as much detail as possible at the outset to minimize revisions.

Copyright & Usage

  • The work May Not be Used Commercially without written permission.
  • Credit or a mention may be included when posting on social media.
  • I reserve the right to post the work to my portfolio unless there is an NDA in place.
  • Resale or claiming the work as your own is strictly prohibited.

Cancellation & Refund

  • Cancellation before the line art is completed: 50% refund.
  • After the line art is approved: no refunds.
  • I reserve the right to refuse an order without providing a reason.
  • Turnaround time is subject to change — you will be notified if there are any delays.

What I Do

  • OC (Original Character), fan art, game/anime characters.
  • Portraits, couples, groups (max. 3 characters per order).
  • Stickers and social media avatars.
  • Semi-gore, light horror, or Light NSFW content (please discuss first).

What I Don't Do

  • NSFW content / explicit adult content.
  • Content that contains hate speech or discrimination.
  • Works created for NFT purposes without specific discussion.
  • Content that infringes on third-party copyrights.
